Potato & Leek Soup

A winter treat

I like soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 large leeks, white and pale green parts, rinsed and chopped roughly
  • 1 quart vege stock
  • 0.75 lb medium russet potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 0.5 cup heavy cream
  • 0.5 tsp fresh ground nutmeg
  • sliced chives or scallions to serve

Steps

  1. Melt butter in large saucepan or dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add leeks, reduce heat to low, and cook with frequent stirring until leeks are very soft, but not browned.
  3. Add stock, potatoes, and bay leaf. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil over high heat, reduce to a gentle simmer, cover, and cook until potatoes are very tender.
  5. Add heavy cream and buttermilk to the pot. Discard the bay leaf.
  6. Blend soup, taking care not to over blend. Use slow speed, and if possible break up potatoes manually.
  7. Whisk in grated nutmeg, and serve with chives or scallions on top.
