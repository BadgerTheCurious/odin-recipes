The Best Chilli Recipe

Spicy and delicious!

Mandatory Recipe Story

I love chilli and it's great in every season. Give me more chilli.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 red capsicum, diced
  • 1 (4 oz) can mild green chiles
  • 1 medium sweet potato, cut to ~cm cubes
  • 2.5 tbsp mild chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 0.5 tsp dried oregano
  • 0.25 tsp garlic powder
  • 0.25 tsp paprika
  • 0.25 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 0.75 cup vegetarian broth
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (15 oz) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained

Steps

  1. Place oil in large pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add in garlic, onion, diced carrot, red bell pepper, cubed sweet potato and green chilles.
  3. Saute for 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Add in chili powder, cumin, oregano, garlic powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt and black pepper; stir for about 30 seconds.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, broth, black beans, and kidney beans.
  6. Bring to a boil, then back heat off and simmer 30-40 minutes, until chilli thickens.
  7. Taste and adust seasonings.
