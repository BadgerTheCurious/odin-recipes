Brownie Mix

Chocolate.

Ingredients

For the mix

  • 285 g powdered sugar
  • 85 g finely chopped dark chocolate
  • 63 g all-purpose flour
  • 63 g cocoa powder
  • 30 g malted milk powder
  • 1.25 tsp salt
  • 0.75 tsp espresso powder
  • 50 g refined coconut oil (should be solid, but creamy)

For the brownies

  • 0.25 cup water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

For the mix

  1. Combine all ingredients except coconut oil in a food processor.
  2. Grind until the chocolate disappears into a fine, powdery mix, and there a no longer any chunks in the bowl.
  3. Add the coconut oil and pulse until combined.
  4. Transfer the mix to an airtight contianter and store at cool room temperature up to a year.

For the brownies

  1. Place oven rack in lower-middle position and preheat to 180 C
  2. Add water, eggs and vanilla extract to the mix and stir to well-combined.
  3. Scrape into prepared pan and bake until brownies are glossy, puffed and firm, but still squishy.
  4. Baking time is approx. 35 minutes, but varies strongly by pan.
  5. Allow brownies to cool at least 15 minutes before serving.
